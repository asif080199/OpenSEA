Detailed Description

The Pierson-Moskowitz Spectrum.

This is a wave spectrum based on the Pierson-Moskowitz formula. It is a one parameter spectrum, and depends on wind speed. The spectrum uses the characteristic wind speed. This spectrum assumes a wind speed measured at a height of 10 m above mean sea level. The original wave spectra calculated by Pierson and Moskowitz used wind measurements at a height of 19.5 m above mean sea level. The input wind speed was adjusted to account for this difference.
